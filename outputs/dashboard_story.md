# Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary
The dashboard shows that the retail business has strong sales volume of ₹21.70 Cr and profit of ₹3.33 Cr, with an overall profit margin of 15.3%. The largest sales opportunity is concentrated in the South region, Technology category, Home Office segment and Organic acquisition channel. The main risks are high returns in Furniture, longer delivery times in Standard Class shipping and possible margin pressure from heavy discounting.

## What Is Performing Well
South is the strongest region with ₹6.47 Cr in sales. Technology is the highest-profit category with ₹2.80 Cr in profit. Organic channel generates the highest sales, showing that the business has strong baseline demand without relying only on paid promotion.

## What Is Underperforming
East has the lowest regional sales at ₹4.89 Cr. Office Supplies has a smaller sales and profit contribution compared with Technology and Furniture. This does not automatically mean Office Supplies is a problem, but it suggests that leadership should not expect it to drive the same growth as Technology.

## Visible Risks
Furniture has the highest return rate among major categories at 7.7%. Standard Class has the longest delivery time at 4.7 days on average. Heavy discounting should be monitored because discounts can lift sales while reducing profit quality.

## Visible Opportunities
Technology should be prioritized for inventory planning, campaign focus and upsell opportunities. South can be used as a benchmark for regional playbooks. Home Office customers are valuable because they generate the highest segment-level sales, but the segment also requires return monitoring.

## Recommended Business Actions
1. Protect Technology inventory and campaign visibility because it is the strongest profit driver.
2. Investigate Furniture returns and identify whether the causes are product quality, delivery damage, wrong expectations or shipping delay.
3. Review East region performance and test targeted campaigns or operational improvements.
4. Apply discount limits by category and segment instead of using broad discounting everywhere.
5. Reduce delivery delay in Standard Class for high-value or high-risk categories.

## Limitations of the Dashboard
The dashboard explains patterns in sales, profit, returns and delivery performance, but it does not prove causation. For example, a high return rate in a category does not by itself prove that shipping is the cause. Additional customer feedback, return reasons and campaign cost data would improve decision-making.

## Suggested Next Analysis
The next analysis should include customer lifetime value, campaign ROI, product-level return reasons, inventory stockout data and repeat purchase behavior. These would help leadership move from descriptive monitoring to predictive decision-making.
